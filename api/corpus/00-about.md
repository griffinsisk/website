## About

Griffin Sisk is a Senior Sales Engineer at CloudZero, where he runs technical
evaluations and helps customers build cost models across cloud, Kubernetes, and
AI spend. He has spent six years in technical pre-sales at VMware, Broadcom, and
CloudZero — earning trust with engineering teams, articulating complex value to
executives, and winning competitive deals.

That work pulled him into building with AI himself. He has shipped tools his
team uses every week: an assistant that answers product questions live on
customer calls, a pipeline that turns scattered market news into a weekly
digest, and a public corpus that helps AI coding agents write correct
cost-allocation code. Most of what he knows about context design and failure
modes came from watching these break and fixing them.

His working philosophy: start from the real problem, be honest about what
works, keep iterating. The building makes him sharper in the room with
engineers, and the time with customers tells him what's actually worth
building.

Interests outside work: travel, health and wellness, AI and technology,
photography and videography, animal welfare. Education: University of New
Hampshire (2014–2018), Bachelor of Business and Communications; played Men's
D1 Rugby.
